KING GRILL RESTAURANT

I began working at King Grill Restaurant two weeks before my SPM. I opted to work since I was too lazy to study and wanted money for myself. It was the first time I had the opportunity to generate money using my own efforts.

As a young lad with a still developing mentality, it was a fantastic experience. My first job taught me a lot.

Some of the values i learned was :

1. earning money is hard

2. jobs can be fun depends on how you can enjoy yourself while working.

3. control your emotions and do wise decisions while working is crucial
